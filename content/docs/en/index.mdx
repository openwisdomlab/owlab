## Content Architecture

### Three-Layer Structure

Every module follows the same three-layer structure, serving different depths of learning:

| Layer | Name | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|
| **Core** | Core | Essential knowledge and key principles for quick understanding |
| **Extended** | Extend | Deep topics and best practices for advanced study |
| **Evidence** | Evidence | Research support and references for academic verification |

### Knowledge System

- **Frontier Philosophy**: 4 core philosophy modules (L01-L04), tracking cutting-edge research
- **Knowledge Base**: 9 core modules (M01-M09), covering the full lifecycle of innovation learning spaces
- **Resources & Tools**: Practical tools and resources, the intelligent bridge from philosophy to practice
