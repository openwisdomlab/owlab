# Certification Assessment Standards

## Overview

The OWL educator certification system establishes a clear professional development pathway from apprentice to expert. Each level represents a distinct set of competencies, assessed through multiple modalities to ensure both rigor and authenticity.

**Certification Levels**:
```text
E0 Apprentice → E1 Certified → E2 Lead → E3 Expert
```

---

## 1. Certification System Overview

### 1.1 Certification Levels

| Level | Title | Description | Typical Timeline |
|-------|-------|-------------|-----------------|
| **E0** | Apprentice Educator | Learning the role; supervised practice | 0-6 months |
| **E1** | Certified Educator | Independent facilitation capability | 6-12 months |
| **E2** | Lead Educator | Curriculum design and team leadership | 1-2 years |
| **E3** | Expert Educator | System design and network contribution | 3+ years |

### 1.2 Certification Principles

| Principle | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Evidence-based** | Assessment based on demonstrated competency, not seniority |
| **Multi-modal** | Multiple assessment methods (observation, portfolio, interview) |
| **Growth-oriented** | Focus on development, not gatekeeping |
| **Transparent** | Clear criteria shared with candidates in advance |
| **Peer-involved** | Peer review as part of the assessment process |

## 2. E0 Apprentice Educator

### 2.1 Application Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|------------|---------|
| **Background** | Interest in maker education; any professional background |
| **Safety** | C0 safety certification complete |
| **Commitment** | Minimum 40 hours over 3 months |
| **References** | One reference from OWL community |

### 2.2 Assessment Content

| Domain | Criteria | Weight |
|--------|----------|--------|
| **Attitude** | Curiosity, openness, growth mindset | 30% |
| **Basic skills** | Familiarity with at least one tool domain | 25% |
| **Safety awareness** | Proper safety practices | 25% |
| **Communication** | Can communicate with learners effectively | 20% |

### 2.3 Assessment Methods

| Method | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Interview** | 30-minute conversation about motivation and philosophy |
| **Observation** | Observed assisting during one session |
| **Safety quiz** | Written safety assessment |

### 2.4 E0 Period Requirements

| Activity | Required |
|----------|----------|
| Attend sessions as assistant | 10+ sessions |
| Complete one self-directed project | Yes |
| Maintain reflection journal | Weekly entries |
| Receive mentor feedback | Monthly |

## 3. E1 Certified Educator

### 3.1 Application Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|------------|---------|
| **E0 completion** | At least 3 months as E0 |
| **Hours** | 80+ hours of facilitation experience |
| **Safety** | C1 certification for relevant equipment |
| **Portfolio** | Documentation of 3+ facilitated sessions |

### 3.2 Assessment Rubric

| Dimension | Developing (1) | Competent (2) | Proficient (3) | Score |
|-----------|----------------|---------------|-----------------|-------|
| **Facilitation** | Follows scripts; limited learner interaction | Adapts to learner needs; good engagement | Creates responsive, dynamic learning experiences | /3 |
| **Technical** | Basic tool competency; needs support | Confident with multiple tools; can troubleshoot | Deep expertise; can improvise solutions | /3 |
| **Safety** | Follows safety procedures | Proactively identifies and addresses risks | Builds safety culture; trains others | /3 |
| **Communication** | Clear basic instructions | Adapts communication to audience | Inspires curiosity; masterful questioning | /3 |
| **Reflection** | Can identify what happened | Analyzes why and how to improve | Systematically improves practice | /3 |

**Pass threshold**: Average score >= 2.0; no dimension below 1.5

### 3.3 Assessment Methods

| Method | Description | Weight |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| **Teaching demonstration** | Facilitate a 30-minute session observed by assessors | 40% |
| **Portfolio review** | Documentation of facilitated sessions with reflections | 30% |
| **Interview** | Discussion of educational philosophy and scenarios | 20% |
| **Peer feedback** | Feedback from fellow educators and learners | 10% |

### 3.4 Teaching Demonstration Rubric

| Criterion | Indicators |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Opening** | Engages learners; clear objectives; creates psychological safety |
| **Facilitation** | Asks open questions; supports without over-directing; manages time |
| **Differentiation** | Adapts to different skill levels; multiple pathways |
| **Safety** | Integrates safety naturally; models safe practices |
| **Closing** | Meaningful reflection; celebrates learning; previews next steps |

## 4. E2 Lead Educator

### 4.1 Application Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|------------|---------|
| **E1 certification** | Active E1 for at least 12 months |
| **Hours** | 200+ hours of independent facilitation |
| **Curriculum** | Designed at least 2 original courses/workshops |
| **Mentoring** | Mentored at least 1 E0 educator |
| **Safety** | C2 certification for at least one equipment type |

### 4.2 Assessment Rubric

| Dimension | Competent (2) | Proficient (3) | Expert (4) | Score |
|-----------|---------------|-----------------|------------|-------|
| **Curriculum Design** | Effective learning experiences with clear objectives | Innovative curricula aligned with 3E framework | Published, replicated curricula with evidence of impact | /4 |
| **Leadership** | Supports team members effectively | Develops others' capabilities; facilitates growth | Builds high-performing teams; shapes culture | /4 |
| **Innovation** | Applies established methods creatively | Develops new approaches to challenges | Contributes to the field of maker education | /4 |
| **Community** | Active community participant | Community builder and connector | Network-level contributor | /4 |
| **Impact** | Positive learner outcomes | Measurable, documented impact | Systemic impact beyond own classroom | /4 |

**Pass threshold**: Average score >= 2.5; no dimension below 2.0

### 4.3 Assessment Methods

| Method | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Portfolio review** | Comprehensive portfolio of curricula, projects, reflections |
| **Curriculum presentation** | Present original curriculum to assessment panel |
| **Peer review** | Review by 2 E2+ educators |
| **Learner impact evidence** | Data on learner outcomes and growth |

---

## 5. E3 Expert Educator

### 5.1 Application Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|------------|---------|
| **E2 certification** | Active E2 for at least 24 months |
| **Hours** | 500+ total facilitation hours |
| **Contribution** | Published curriculum or research; conference presentation |
| **Network impact** | Demonstrated impact beyond own node |
| **Mentoring** | Mentored 3+ educators through E0→E1 |

### 5.2 Assessment Methods

| Method | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Expert panel review** | Presentation and Q&A with E3-level panel |
| **Portfolio defense** | Comprehensive career portfolio with impact evidence |
| **External references** | References from outside OWL network |

### 5.3 E3 Assessment Focus Areas

| Area | Assessment Question |
|------|-------------------|
| **Vision** | Does the candidate have a clear, compelling educational vision? |
| **Innovation** | Has the candidate contributed original ideas to the field? |
| **Impact** | Can the candidate demonstrate measurable, sustained impact? |
| **Leadership** | Does the candidate develop others and build capacity? |
| **Network** | Does the candidate contribute to the broader OWL network? |

## 6. Certification Process

### 6.1 Application Process

```text
1. Self-assessment against criteria
2. Submit application with portfolio
3. Application screening (1-2 weeks)
4. Assessment scheduling (2-4 weeks)
5. Assessment (varies by level)
6. Results communication (1-2 weeks)
7. Certification issuance
```

### 6.2 Timeline

| Level | Application to Result | Assessment Duration |
|-------|----------------------|-------------------|
| E0 | 2-4 weeks | 1 session |
| E1 | 4-6 weeks | 1-2 sessions |
| E2 | 6-8 weeks | 2-3 sessions |
| E3 | 8-12 weeks | Panel review |

### 6.3 Application Materials Checklist

| Material | E0 | E1 | E2 | E3 |
|----------|----|----|----|----|
| Application form | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| CV/resume | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Philosophy statement | - | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Portfolio | - | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| References | 1 | 2 | 3 | 5 |
| Learner impact data | - | - | Yes | Yes |
| Publications/presentations | - | - | Optional | Required |

## 7. Renewal and Review

### 7.1 Renewal Requirements

| Level | Validity | Renewal Requirements |
|-------|----------|---------------------|
| E0 | N/A | Progress to E1 or exit |
| E1 | 2 years | 40 hours facilitation + 8 hours PD |
| E2 | 3 years | 80 hours facilitation + 16 hours PD + 1 new curriculum |
| E3 | 5 years | Active network contribution + peer review |

### 7.2 Renewal Process

1. Submit renewal application with evidence
2. Self-assessment against current criteria
3. Brief review by certification committee
4. Renewal or upgrade recommendation

### 7.3 Annual Professional Development

| Activity | PD Hours |
|----------|----------|
| Attend workshop/conference | 4-8 per event |
| Complete online course | 2-4 per course |
| Publish article or resource | 4-8 per publication |
| Mentor an E0 educator | 8 per mentee |
| Design new curriculum | 8 per curriculum |

## 8. Certification Management

### 8.1 Certificate Management

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Digital certificate** | Verifiable digital credential |
| **Badge** | Digital badge for online profiles |
| **Registry** | Public registry of certified educators |

### 8.2 Certification Revocation

Grounds for revocation:
- Safety violations
- Ethical misconduct
- Failure to maintain requirements
- False information in application

### 8.3 Appeals Mechanism

- Submit written appeal within 30 days
- Review by independent committee
- Decision within 30 days
- Final appeal to network governance
