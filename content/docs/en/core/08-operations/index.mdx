## Building Standards

<Callout type="info">
The core of M08 is "keeping inquiry alive." Assessment criteria are designed around **whether processes are replicable, whether service is stable, and whether improvement is continuous**, letting great operations "invisibly" support the release of creativity.
</Callout>

### Core Principles

| ID | Core Principle | One-Liner | Practice Signals |
|----|---------------|-----------|-----------------|
| **N1** | Invisible Operations | Great operations let people see only the exciting parts, not the processes | User experience is smooth with no perceived barriers |
| **N2** | Replicable Processes | Newcomers can ramp up quickly; tacit knowledge made explicit | SOPs exist; new staff can handle duty independently within 3 days |
| **N3** | User-Centered | Design services around user experience | Service satisfaction continuously tracked |
| **N4** | Data-Driven | Make decisions with data, not intuition | Operations dashboard exists; data reviewed regularly |
| **N5** | Continuous Improvement | Improvement is a continuous process, not a one-off project | PDSA cycle operational |

### Mandatory Standards (10 items)

| ID | Standard | Why It Matters | Quantitative Metric | Tier |
|----|----------|---------------|---------------------|------|
| **S1** | Core SOPs Established | Foundation for replicable processes | Opening/closing/event execution SOPs 100% established | Foundation |
| **S2** | Duty Log Records | Operations are traceable | Duty logs 100% completed | Foundation |
| **S3** | Booking System Operational | Orderly resource management | Booking system functioning normally; no-show rate &lt;10% | Foundation |
| **S4** | Open Day Execution | Window for community connection | >= 1 open day event per month | Foundation |
| **S5** | Basic Member Services | User operations with a clear playbook | New member welcome contact within 24 hours | Foundation |
| **S6** | Member Retention Management | Building long-term relationships | Monthly active rate >= 50%; renewal rate >= 60% | Growth |
| **S7** | Operational Data Tracking | Data-driven decisions | Core metrics updated weekly | Growth |
| **S8** | PDSA Cycle Operational | Continuous improvement mechanism | >= 1 weekly retrospective with improvement records | Growth |
| **S9** | Service Satisfaction | User experience assurance | Service satisfaction >= 4.0/5.0 | Excellence |
| **S10** | SOP Iterative Updates | Continuous process optimization | Core SOPs updated >= 1 time per year | Excellence |

### Parameter Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Healthy Range | Warning Signal |
|--------|-----------|--------------|----------------|
| **Monthly Member Activity Rate** | Active members / Total members x 100% | 50%-70% | &lt;40% members may be "dormant" |
| **First Experience Conversion Rate** | Became member after first experience / First-time visitors x 100% | 20%-40% | &lt;15% experience design may have issues |
| **Service Response Time** | Average response time for member inquiries | &lt;4 hours | >24 hours service may be lagging |

### Extension Patterns

| Pattern | Applicable Context | Core Practice | Expected Outcome |
|---------|-------------------|--------------|-----------------|
| **Operations Retrospective** | Strengthening continuous improvement | Fixed weekly team retrospective using the PDSA framework to analyze problems and improvements | Problems discovered promptly; improvement happens continuously |
| **Member Ambassador Program** | Activating veteran members | Invite active members to become "ambassadors," participating in new member onboarding and event support | Member belonging strengthened; operational burden reduced |

**Monthly Member Lifecycle Health Check**

```text
+-------------------------------------------------------------+
|  Member Lifecycle Check                                      |
+-------------------------------------------------------------+
|  Acquisition Stage                                           |
|  [] Open day executed >= 1 time this month                   |
|  [] First experience conversion rate >= 20%                  |

|  [] New members receive welcome contact within 24 hours      |

+-------------------------------------------------------------+

|  [] Members upgraded or referred new members                 |
+-------------------------------------------------------------+
```
